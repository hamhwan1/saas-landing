Build a modern SaaS landing page for a product called "Yettey".

Yettey is an AI-powered Digital Asset Management (DAM) platform that helps users manage media files and turn them into content using AI.

--------------------------------------------------

🎯 Core Concept

The product positioning is:

1. Manage your media (DAM)
2. Understand your media (AI analysis)
3. Turn media into content (AI transformation)

This flow must be clearly reflected in the landing page.

--------------------------------------------------

🎨 Design Style

- Dark theme (primary)
- Modern SaaS UI (similar to Vercel, Notion, OpusClip)
- Clean, minimal, high-end 느낌
- Smooth animations (fade-in, hover effects)
- Strong typography hierarchy
- Card-based layout

--------------------------------------------------

📌 Header (Navigation)

Sticky header with:

Left:
- Logo (Yettey)

Center:
- Product (dropdown)
    - Digital Asset Management
    - AI Content Creation
    - Video Transformation
    - Collaboration

- Solutions (dropdown)
    - For Marketers
    - For Content Creators
    - For Teams / Agencies

- Resources (dropdown)
    - Blog
    - Guides
    - Use Cases

- Pricing

Right:
- Login
- Primary CTA button: "Get Started"

--------------------------------------------------
